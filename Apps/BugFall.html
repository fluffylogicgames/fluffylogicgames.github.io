BugFall

Crystal cracking, bug saving arcade action! Rescue the bugs from the clutches of the evil Crypt King. Each bug rescued helps you with a unique perk and there’s a perk for every play style. Can you rescue them all? Get going and foil the Crypt King!

  • UNLOCK unique bugs and perks
  • DISCOVER 4 habitats each with their own special insect inhabitants
  • DEFEAT the hideous crypt worms
  • BEAT your high score
  • DODGE the falling skulls
  • QUEST for rewards and find the secret bugs
  • CUTE and crazy insect characters
  • SHARE your bug list with friends
